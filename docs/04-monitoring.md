# Lab 4: Monitoring & Analytics

## Introduction
Analyze the AI traffic in your organization.

## Steps
1. Navigate to **Analytics** -> **Gateway**.
2. Filter by "Application" and look for AI categories.
3. Review "DLP" logs to see blocked sensitive data.
4. Setup an "Alert" to notify security teams when a DLP violation occurs.

## Verification
- View the dashboard and confirm your test blocks are appearing in the charts.
